Cream Cheese and Crab Sushi Rolls


Description

Indulge in the delightful fusion of creamy and savory flavors with these Cream Cheese and Crab Sushi Rolls. Perfectly suited for sushi enthusiasts and newcomers alike, these rolls combine the delicate sweetness of crab with the rich, smooth texture of cream cheese, all wrapped in a sheet of nori and perfectly seasoned sushi rice. Each bite delivers a harmonious blend of taste and texture, making it an irresistible treat. Whether enjoyed as an appetizer, a light meal, or a party dish, these sushi rolls are sure to impress with their simple yet elegant composition. Enjoy the fresh, clean flavors and the satisfying contrast of the tender crab and creamy filling, all wrapped up in a convenient, bite-sized roll.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 sheets nori seaweed sheets
  • 2 pieces imitation crab legs
  • Â½ (3 ounce) package cream cheese, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring rice and water to a boil in a saucepan over high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender, and the liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes. Stir in rice vinegar and salt. Allow to cool completely.
  3. Lay out seaweed sheets. Moisten hands with water, then spread cooled rice evenly on each sheet, leaving a 1/2-inch gap along one edge, lengthwise. Arrange strips of cucumber, imitation crabmeat, and cream cheese on top of the rice in a straight line along the side opposite the gap. Roll up sushi from the toppings to the seaweed sheet's exposed end.
  4. Using a sharp wet knife, slice each roll into 5 or 6 pieces. Serve with minced ginger on the side.
